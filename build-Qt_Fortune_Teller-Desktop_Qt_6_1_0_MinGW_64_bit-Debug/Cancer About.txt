You are easily hurt by the slights of others.
You are very complex, sometimes appearing extremely strong and at other times to be as vulnerable as a child.
You are remarkably good at accumulating things; indeed, you can be unwilling to throw anything out, even relationships that have passed your use-by date.
You are generally over-anxious in financial matters, and make great efforts to gather in money.
You have unusual ups and downs in your early life and so you are compelled to work to keep ahead, but once you get on your feet you keep there.
If channeled in the right direction, your enormous sensitivity can become a great source of strength.
Once you overcome your touchiness and master your turbulent emotions, your intellect and imagination enable you to become a success in almost anything you undertake.
Your intuitions are reliable and should be trusted.
You are generally gifted with strong imaginations.
